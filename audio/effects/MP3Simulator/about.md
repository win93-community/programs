# MP3 Simulator

Real-time MP3 damage box to remake 1999 napster bubbly tones.

The tone comes from actual MP3 compression using the classic Blade encoder.
Smeared transients, swishy highs, unstable stereo detail.

- **Bitrate**: Target MP3 bitrate.
- **Quality**: Encoder quality level.
- **Feedback**: Feeds one spectral frame into the next for buzzy resonance.
- **Bias**: Shifts what the encoder throws away.
- **Glitch**: Adds artifacts and broken character.
- **Mix**: Blends dry and wet signals.
- **Band Reassignment**: Columns are output bands, rows are input bands.
