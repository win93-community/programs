# Karaoke Player

A karaoke web player with speed control.  


The checkbox/toggle preserves the pitch of the playback. Right-click on the speed bar to reset the playback speed.  


Based on [ltucker/html5_karaoke](https://github.com/ltucker/html5_karaoke)  
Windows93 port by [Jankenpopp](http://jankenpopp.com)
